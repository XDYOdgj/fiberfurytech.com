Coby CBPAC1080H 4-in-1 Portable Air Conditioner

The Coby CBPAC1080H is a powerful 4-in-1 unit that combines air conditioning, heating, dehumidification, and fan functions into one convenient device. Perfect for large rooms, it offers up to 15,000 BTU cooling and heating capacity, ensuring your space stays comfortable no matter the season. Whether you're cooling your home during summer or heating your bedroom in winter, this unit delivers reliable performance while keeping the air fresh and dry.

Key Features

  • 15,000 BTU Cooling & Heating Capacity: Efficiently cools and heats rooms up to 775 square feet, helping you maintain ideal indoor temperatures year-round.
  • 4-in-1 Functionality: From AC and heater to dehumidifier and fan, it's the perfect all-in-one solution for versatile climate control.
  • Adjustable Cooling & Fan Speeds: Customize your comfort with three-speed cooling and three-speed fan options, allowing for precise temperature management.
  • 24-Hour Timer & Remote Control: Convenience at your fingertips with features like a built-in timer and remote functionality to manage your unit from anywhere.
  • Durable Mobility: Equipped with four casters and side handles, the Coby CBPAC1080H is easy to move from room to room, making it ideal for apartments or temporary spaces.

Why Choose the Coby CBPAC1080H?

This portable AC unit is more than just a cooling device. With built-in dehumidifier and heater functions, it helps improve indoor comfort and air quality, especially in humid or cold climates. The intuitive LED display keeps you informed about the current settings and performance, while the auto water evaporation system ensures quiet and efficient operation. Designed for renters, homeowners, or anyone seeking a hassle-free cooling and heating system, the Coby CBPAC1080H is the smart choice for modern living.
