Stay Cool, Stay Comfortable with the Midea Portable Air Conditioner

The Midea portable AC is a versatile cooling solution designed to bring relief to your home, bedroom, or office. It can efficiently cool spaces up to 175 square feet, making it perfect for renters, small spaces, or areas without access to central AC.

With an 8000 BTU ASHRAE rating and a 5300 BTU SACC rating, this unit ensures you stay at an optimal temperature while maintaining fresh and clean indoor air. The built-in dehumidifier mode helps fight humidity, while the fan setting provides gentle airflow without cooling, perfect for warmer days when you just need a breeze.

Designed with convenience in mind, the Midea portable AC comes with an easy-to-install window kit and a 5ft exhaust hose. Simply set it up in your window and start enjoying instant cooling. Its digital LED display offers clear and intuitive controls, while the remote control lets you manage settings from across the room, ensuring effortless operation.

The unit also features a removable and washable air filter, which can be cleaned weekly to maintain a healthier indoor environment. Whether you're cooling a room, reducing humidity, or just needing fresh air, the Midea portable AC is built to handle it all seamlessly and effectively.
