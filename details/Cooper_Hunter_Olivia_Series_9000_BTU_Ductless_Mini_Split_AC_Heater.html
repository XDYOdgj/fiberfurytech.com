Cooper & Hunter Olivia Series

Experience year-round comfort with this advanced ductless mini split system. Designed for both cooling and heating, the Olivia Series offers superior energy efficiency and reliable performance, making it a smart choice for homeowners.

Efficient Cooling and Heating

The Cooper & Hunter Olivia Series delivers powerful performance with a 9000 BTU capacity, perfectly suited for moderate-sized rooms. Whether it's a sweltering summer day or a chilly winter night, this system keeps your space comfortable without compromising efficiency.

Energy Savings with 23 SEER

Thanks to its high-efficiency 23 SEER inverter system, the Olivia Series reduces energy consumption and helps you save on utility bills. This is not just an air conditioner—it's an air conditioner that works smarter.

Complete Installation Kit

With everything you need included, the Olivia Series makes installation seamless and efficient. The full installation kit features 16ft pre-flared and insulated copper line sets, communication wires, and other essential components, ensuring a hassle-free setup process.

Reliable and Safe Performance

This ductless mini split system is ETL Intertek and AHRI certified for top quality and safety. The 115V/60Hz/1Ph design ensures compatibility with standard U.S. electrical systems, making it a dependable and trouble-free solution for your home.

Minimalist Wall Mount Design

The Olivia Series features a sleek wall mount design that blends effortlessly with your home's interior while maximizing available space. It's the perfect addition for those who value both function and style.

Professional Support and Installation

Backed by U.S.-based technicians, the Olivia Series ensures you receive expert support and a reliable installation experience. This system is designed for professional installation, which means optimal performance and long-term satisfaction from day one.
