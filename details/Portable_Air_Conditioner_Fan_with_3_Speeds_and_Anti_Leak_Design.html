Stay Cool on the Go with the USB Mini Air Cooler

This compact and lightweight portable air cooler is the perfect solution for keeping your space cool without the hassle of water tanks. Once you freeze the included ice packs, they can provide up to 10 hours of continuous cooling, making it ideal for hot summer days, office use, or even camping trips.

Enjoy Energy-Efficient Cooling, Anywhere, Anytime

Designed for versatility, this USB-powered mini air cooler is reliable whether you're at home, in the office, or traveling. Its small size allows for easy placement on desks, in tents, or in small rooms. The unit is powered by a USB connection, making it compatible with computer ports, car chargers, and portable power banks for convenience. With three adjustable fan speeds, you can customize the airflow to suit your needs for a quiet, energy-saving cooling experience.
